FIRST
        FIRST stands for For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology. Founded by Dean Kamen, it is considered by him as his greatest creation. FIRST is an organization that promotes STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) to students all around the world by inspiring them to design and build robots for a competition. FIRST hosts four competitions: Jr. FLL, FLL, FTC, and FRC.
FTC
        FIRST Tech Challenge, or FTC, is a high school level competition created to inspire young minds around the world to design and build robots that will compete against other robots to complete a challenge. In September, teams worldwide are given a challenge that their robot must complete. The teams must use a set list of materials to build a robot to complete that challenge.
